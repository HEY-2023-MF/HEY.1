Our purposes

Mision

Offer the user a tool to be able to organize their student and personal life, through an easy and optimal system for their greater comfort when doing so.

Vision

Our vision is to help people, especially the students of the ¡Supérate! to organize your day to day, and we also want to expand this to other fields or environments in which it is required.

Objective

Contribute positively to the young people of the Center in order to improve their methods of organization of time and tasks, in turn to train an extra help so that they maintain a good practice and help them in their day to day.

Our colors

Black

The color black is associated with death and suffering, mourning, the darkness of darkness, the terror of the unknown, magic and mystery, but also solemnity and trust.

Dark purple

Dark purple is a color that is associated with power, nobility, luxury, ambition and loyalty. It can normally be related as independence, dignity, wisdom and creativity.

Light purple

Light purple is a color that conveys sensitivity, delicacy, elegance, luxury or spirituality and is also associated with mystery, mysticism, melancholy, dreams and magic.
